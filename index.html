Print & Digital

As a graphic designer, I constantly toggle between the print medium and computational tools. This website is an ongoing exploration of these blended realities where the tactile qualities of print meet the dynamic nature of the digital. The platform functions not only as a space for creative coding but also as a diary of my learning journey with p5.js where every click, glitch, and interaction becomes a moment of discovery.

This project is part of the RISD Spring 2025 Extended Reality and Shared Future CTC class. I would like to thank Livia Foldes for her insightful mentorship, unwavering support throughout this journey, and for introducing and encouraging me to begin my coding journey.
